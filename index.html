Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an app to use FurTagPH?

No app needed. Any smartphone camera can scan the QR code and open the pet profile instantly—making it easy for anyone to help.

What happens when someone scans my pet's tag?

They'll see your pet's public profile (pet photo, name, medical notes) and secure contact options (phone or email) to reach you directly.

Is my contact information public?

Your details are only shown to the finder after the tag is scanned, not publicly indexed. You are in control of what information is displayed.

How durable is the QR tag?

Our tags are made from high-quality, non-toxic materials, designed to be waterproof, weather-resistant, and durable for everyday pet life.
